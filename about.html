About TNR HR

Your Strategic HR Partner

About Us

TNR HR Business Solutions Pty Ltd is a full service HR and Labour Law compliance partner providing strategic and operational people solutions to businesses across South Africa. We support organisations of all sizes, from small businesses with five employees to enterprises employing over 2,000 staff members, delivering expertise across HR, ER, IR, Payroll, Labour Law Compliance, Training, Recruitment, Labour Broking, Recruitment of People Living with Disabilities, and Employee Wellness Programmes.

What sets us apart is our hands on, embedded approach. We provide the full experience of a dedicated HR department led by senior HR professionals, offering daily virtual support combined with structured on site visits. We do not operate on an ad hoc basis. We partner with you consistently, becoming an extension of your leadership team.

We take time to understand your industry, operational challenges, project requirements, and labour budget to implement people management solutions aligned with your business vision and current labour legislation. At TNR HR Business Solutions, we do not simply advise. We partner, implement, and deliver measurable results.

Our mission is to deliver end to end HR and Labour Law solutions that protect, strengthen, and support businesses across South Africa.
