Recruitment

Finding the right talent to drive your business forward

Head-Hunting Services

  • Suitable for Specialist, Senior, and Executive positions where candidates are not always on the employment market
  • Suitable for hard-to-fill positions or positions where the organisation has had suitable placement or retention challenges in the past
  • Suitable for new positions in the organisation where the organisation understands the role they are trying to fill but may not necessarily have knowledge and feel of that particular area of the market
  • The service is offered at a 12% placement fee
  • 30% of the placement fee is paid up-front to initiate the recruitment process, is non-refundable, and does not form part of the guarantee terms. It is calculated based on the budget for the position
  • 70% of the placement fee is payable within 7-days of the start-date of the successful incumbent as an employee of the organisation, to which fee the guarantee terms are applicable

Specialist and Management Recruitment

  • This is normal recruitment for vacant positions
  • We specialise in support and management positions
  • We are versed in Manufacturing, Engineering and Construction, Legal, Financial Services, Telecommunications, and Non-Profit industries
  • The service is offered at a 12% placement fee
  • The placement fee is payable within 7-days of the start-date of the successful incumbent as an employee of the organisation, to which fee the guarantee terms are applicable

Labour-Broking and Temporary Employment Services

  • Suitable for project based, month-to-month, fixed-term, and casual employment of specialists, support staff, and operations staff
  • We appoint the employees and are liable for legislative and regulatory compliance whilst the organisation is liable for the operational management
  • Legislative and regulatory compliance includes employer contributions, and leave pay
  • Operational management includes health and safety, PPE, and training
  • We are versed in Manufacturing, Engineering and Construction, Legal, Financial Services, Telecommunications, and Non-Profit industries
  • We charge a service fee of 40% for each placement, on a monthly basis, for the duration of the employee placement

Bulk Recruitment

  • Suitable for start-up, peak season recruitment, internships and learnerships, and etc.
  • We are versed in Manufacturing, Engineering and Construction, Legal, Financial Services, Telecommunications, and Non-Profit industries
  • The service is offered at a 10% placement fee per successful incumbent
  • The placement fee is payable within 7-days of the start-date of the successful incumbent as an employee of the organisation, to which fee the guarantee terms are applicable

People-Living-with-Disabilities Recruitment
